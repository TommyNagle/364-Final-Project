Welcome to NFL Statistics!

In this site, you can search for specific NFL teams and widereceiver players to see their specific statistics.

Navigate to player search to find specific widereceivers and see their information.

Navigate to team search to find specific NFL teams and information about them.

Click on summary statistics to select various criteria for ranking the players.

Use the admin login to log into an authorized admin account and modify the database.
